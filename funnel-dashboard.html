Analyst recommendations
🔴

Critical: 41% drop at sign-up form (Step 2)

The largest single drop-off point is the sign-up form. Four required fields create friction for cold visitors. Recommendation: reduce to email only at Step 2 and collect company/role data post-onboarding. Projected impact: +8–12pp on Step 2 conversion based on B2B SaaS benchmarks.

🟡

High: 70% drop at plan selection (Step 3)

Users who reach Step 3 are already qualified — but only 30% proceed. Session replay data suggests users abandon when they cannot see a feature comparison. Recommendation: add a collapsible feature matrix directly on the plan card.

🟢

Opportunity: Growth plan selected by 61% of converters

The majority of converters choose the higher-value plan without being pushed. This suggests pricing anchoring is working. Recommendation: test a third Enterprise tier to shift Growth plan from "Popular" anchor to middle-tier.

📊

Tracking methodology

All events captured via Amplitude SDK with user identification at sign-up (Step 2). Properties tracked per event: funnel_step, intent, timestamp. Session replay enabled at 100% sample rate. Funnel analysis performed in Amplitude Charts → Funnel Analysis, with conversion window set to 30 minutes.
